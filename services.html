Strategy

Engagement with practice and project leadership is critical to embed effective BIM goals across an organisation. Adoption driven from the top — with clear standards, workflows, and accountability — delivers better projects in less time. We support leaders with the planning, protocols, and audit insight to make that stick.

Strategic work includes planning support, high-level BIM alignment, and office-wide protocol development.
