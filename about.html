About

Dimension Group is a design and consulting practice built around clear communication and strong documentation

We help clients and project teams move from ideas to coordinated delivery through architectural support, BIM workflows, technical documentation, and hands-on coordination when timelines compress.

Whether you are a homeowner planning an alteration, a developer rolling out a commercial scheme, or a studio needing reliable Revit production or model governance, the goal is the same: decisions you can see on the drawings and in the model.

Background

The practice draws on professional experience across architecture, BIM management, technical documentation, and project coordination, including international work that sharpened collaboration across schedules, standards, and remote teams.

That mix means we speak the language of planners, consultants, and site teams as well as clients who may be commissioning design support for the first time. You can expect a direct, approachable tone paired with a disciplined approach to files, naming, and deliverables.

How we work

Engagements typically start with a short briefing and alignment on outcomes: what you need to submit, who else is on the team, and what “good” looks like at each milestone. From there we structure work in clear packages: concept, developed, technical, BIM, or coordination, so scope and fees stay transparent.
